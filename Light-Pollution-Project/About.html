What is Light Pollution

The definition of light pollution, also known as photopollution or luminous pollution, is not a new phenomenon. Over the last 50 years, as countries became affluent and urbanized, demand for outdoor lighting increased and light pollution sprawled beyond the city limits and into suburban and rural areas
